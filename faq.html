1. What is Singhvi Marble & Stone ?

We M/s. Singhvi Marble & Stone is a trusted association exceeding expectations in Manufacturing, Supplying and Exporting of blue stone, brown stone and red mandana Stone in India. Our generation office is deliberately situated to guarantee the quickest and best conveyance of the items to all the urban and rural areas.

2. Why should I purchase Kota Stone from here?

Our Kota Stone are of Good Quality and are affordable. It makes difference to lives of thousands of workers, by making a substantial difference in their living standards and personal health.

3. What is the difference between Blue Stone and Brown Stone?

Kota Blue stone comes in various forms – polished tiles, tumbled tiles, natural bricks, cobbles, covings.
Kota Brown stone can be used both in exteriors and interiors, and available in the shade of yellow, green and brown.
